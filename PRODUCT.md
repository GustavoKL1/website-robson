# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Mix of commercial and individual buyers in southern Brazil:

- **Construction companies** — project managers and engineers sourcing containers for job site offices, storage, and worker facilities
- **Industrial buyers** — logistics, warehousing, and manufacturing managers needing durable modular space quickly
- **General public** — homeowners and small business owners seeking affordable, ready-to-use container solutions for land or commercial lots

All users share a context of practical need: they want a functional, durable structure delivered fast without construction delays.

## Product Purpose

Chilante Container sells containers and container modifications (industrial, office, sanitary, and custom modules) across southern Brazil. The site exists to convert visitors into leads by demonstrating credibility, product range, and responsiveness — ultimately driving quote requests and WhatsApp contact.

Success metric: visitor initiates contact (form submission or WhatsApp).

## Brand Personality

**Robust, Confident, Industrial.** The brand communicates strength, reliability, and no-nonsense industrial competence. Tone is direct and factual — no hype, no buzzwords. Every interaction should feel like talking to a knowledgeable factory floor manager.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS templates** — no cookie-cutter corporate layouts, no stock-photo hero sections, no "modern startup" aesthetic. This is heavy industry, not a software product.

## Design Principles

1. **Form follows function** — every element serves the conversion goal. Decoration without purpose is noise.
2. **Industrial honesty** — materials, textures, and colors should feel physical and durable, not digital and airy.
3. **Direct communication** — copy is specific, factual, and concise. No marketing fluff, no empty superlatives.
4. **Trust through transparency** — show real products, real specifications, real pricing. Build confidence that the company delivers what it promises.
5. **Mobile-first, accessibility-first** — buyers access the site from construction sites and phones. Every interaction works on a slow connection with one thumb.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 contrast minimum)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — no critical content depends on animation
- Visible focus indicators on all interactive elements
- Meaningful alt text on all images
- Keyboard-navigable forms and navigation
